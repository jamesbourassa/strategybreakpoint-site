Know when your strategy stops being true.

A Strategy Breakpoint (SBP) is the moment your plan’s assumptions no longer match reality— and continuing “as if nothing changed” becomes the highest-risk choice.

Start with SBP Get updates

Definition

SBP is a decision trigger: identify the assumption that breaks, measure the drift, then rebase strategy to a new representation of the same reality.

When it helps

Product pivots, forecasting, risk governance, model-driven decisions, funding strategy, operations— anywhere incentives favor denial of change.

What this site will become

A short field guide, a lightweight checklist, and case studies. No hype—just a usable breakpoint method.

Canonical naming

Use Strategy Breakpoint (SBP) on first mention; SBP thereafter. Avoid camel-case.
